# W2E — What to Eat | Your AI Foodie Bestie

W2E uses AI to find your next meal without the debate. Match food preferences with your squad and eat happy.

## Key Features

- **Squad Matching** — Group food preference matching so everyone agrees
- **AI Recommendations** — Personalized meal suggestions powered by OpenAI
- **Food Discovery** — Explore restaurants via Google Maps integration
- **Live Location Sharing** — Real-time location with travel time estimates
- **Taste Profiles** — Build your flavor identity over time
- **Rewards & Achievements** — Streaks, badges, and food journey milestones
- **Multi-Language** — Supports 5 languages
- **Cross-Platform** — iOS, Android (React Native/Expo) + Web (React/Vite)
